Our Donate Plan

This is an outline of the things we are doing in sylhet. All of these are from the funds we are recieving from the well-wishers like you.

Food For a family

Foods are scarce due to the disaster. Thats why we are providing some basic foods and water to the diseased families. Till now we have provided food for about 50 families.

Shelter for a family

People are abandoning their home as water levels are rising. Thus a huge demand has been created for a shelter. We are trying our best to provide shelter to the families who have lost their home.

Education for a child

All schools and exams are held off. But still we are trying to provide primary Education for Children between ages 8-14.

Children health at risk

Flood has created a scope for various waterborne disease. In this regard, children of age 1-10 are in the most vulnerable position.
